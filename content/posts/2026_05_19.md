## Plywood

* [Tom Sachs' ode to plywood](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pVxldyIa0Bg) from 2012 is an artful and educational video essay on one of the modern world's underappreciated marvels, plywood.
* The folks at _Design in Practice_ make a [solid case](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=esWbj-FPbV4) for plywood walls over conventional drywall. I am currently in the process of building such a wall for my home office.

## Tech greed

* John Mauriello from the _Design Theory_ channel put out an [excellent piece](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hDvAQf1cnr8) on the mechanisms and systems that create and lead to many of the tech industry's products of questionable value. He elaborates and offers comprehensive analysis of why we often encounter scams, grifts, and solutions looking for problems instead of genuine improvements to our lives.
