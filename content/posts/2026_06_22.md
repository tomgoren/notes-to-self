A tried and true vegetarian side dish, easy to make and eggplant-skeptic friendly.

_Fun fact: The eggplant is botanically classified as a berry!_

## Ingredients

### Equipment

We need:

* A large sheet pan (a.k.a. baking tray, baking sheet, or baking pan)
* A brush
* A bowl

### Eggplants

* **two medium size eggplants**

### Paint

* 1/4 cup Olive oil (~60 ml)
* 2 tablespoons Soy sauce
* 1 tablespoon Granulate garlic
* 1 teaspoon Paprika (optional)

## Preparation

* Heat the oven to 400 °F (~200 °C)

### Eggplant

* Cut off the stem portion from the top of the fruit
* Slice the eggplant length-wise into 1/4 inch slabs (0.625 cm)
* Lay the slices with space in between them on the sheet pan

### Art

* Mix the paint in a bowl
* With a brush, coat the eggplant with a generous layer
* Flip the pieces and paint the other side

## Bake

* Put in the oven for 25 minutes
* It's ready when the glaze is golden brown, and the eggplant is soft
